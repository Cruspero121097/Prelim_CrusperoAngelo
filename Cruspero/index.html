Tourist Spots

Take a look at some of our memorable camping adventures.

  • Bilar Man-made Forest

    This popular tourist destination showcases the essence of environmental conservation, attracting nature lovers and photography enthusiasts alike.

  • Hinagdanan Cave

    Destination for adventure seekers and nature enthusiasts.

  • Tarsier Sanctuary

    Forest where visitors can see the tarsier, a small native primate, in its natural habitat.

  • Loboc River

    The River Cruise was very relaxing and stunning views.

  • Inambacan Cave and Spring

    If you want peace go here! There used to be a ship-shaped mini island on the center of its river but due to earthquake it was damaged.

  • Baclayon Church

    It is one of the Philippines' oldest Catholic churches — structurally original and still with authentic materials.

  • Panglao Island

    The beach is famous for its white sand, clear waters and vibrant marine life. Making it the perfect place to relax and enjoy the view.

  • Mag-aso Falls

    Swing yourself over the water and jump in.

  • Sikatuna's Mirror of the World

    Sikatuna Mirror of the World is a theme park and a botanical garden located in Libjo, Sikatuna, Bahol, Philippines.

  • Chocolate Hills

    Embark on a unique desert camping experience and witness stunning hills.

  • Blood Compact Shrine

    The monument features life-size bronze statues

  • Cadapdapan Rice Terraces- Candijay

    most amazing rice terraces on Bohol. Head to Candijay to see the incredible view for yourself!

  • Can Umantad Falls

    one of the most impressive waterfalls you will find on the incredible island of Bohol, Philippines!

  • Mandahunog Springs

    Mandahunog Spring and Cave is a great place who loves to have fun in the water where you can swim in the natural cold spring waters.
